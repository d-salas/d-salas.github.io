ABOUT

ESP Productions is a full service production and entertainment company handling events of all kinds. We have been in business for over 30 years, and now provide and manage everything necessary to produce the event that you want.

Our focus is on exciting visual and audio design and smooth event flow. We ensure your guests are not only entertained throughout the course of the affair but are delighted and maybe even a little awed.

We perform at over 500 events a year and develop long lasting relationships with our clients. Our results speak for themselves—most of our clients come by referral or the experience of being a guest at an event we’ve produced. We are also regularly recommended by prestigious venues including country clubs, Jewish community centers, museums and restaurants.

 

No matter the event, we'll anticipate your every need.
