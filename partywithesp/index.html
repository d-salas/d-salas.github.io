WHAT WE DO

 

No matter the event, we'll anticipate your every need.

Maybe you want to work around a theme. It could be as elaborate as a Hogwarts bar mitzvah, or as subtle as an evening in Paris dinner for friends. Maybe you just want a certain look, a specific sound. Do you want a noisy crowd scene, with ambiance to match, or the perfect setting for intimate conversation? We ask you questions, getting to know you and acquiring a true sense of what you want your event to be like and how it will reflect who you are. We help develop your concept, then take care of the details that will make it real, from music to visuals to specialty enhancements.

ESP’s job is to make sure that your event happens as you envision it, and that your guests have a wonderful time.

ESP has no sales team–you’ll work with our producers from the beginning. The event will be easy, because the planning and lead up have been handled—by ESP. Your guests will leave knowing that it was your party, nor ours.
