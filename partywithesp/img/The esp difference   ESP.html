The esp difference

ESP’s work is done to “Fortune 500” standards, with a close eye to details. Our job is to make sure that your event happens as you envision it, and that participants leave impressed and perhaps inspired.

Time challenged? Limit your event “to do” list to “Call ESP.” Or work hand-in-hand with your ESP team on every aspect of the event. Choose your venue and caterer, or have us choose for you.

At conferences or seminars, we make production invisible by making it flawless. Screens are framed out properly, wires taped cleanly and sound systems perform perfectly. We can book entertainment for dinner and handle night time entertainment, as well.

For a promotional event, ESP can generate the atmosphere you need. Excitement? Elegance? Technological sophistication? We have the experience to create exactly what you want.

For a gala, fundraiser or a holiday party, we’ll work with you to develop your concept, then handle all the particulars. A night in Monte Carlo night or New Orleans, an old-fashioned carnival or a dance party that gets everyone on the floor—we can fashion whatever fits your company or your cause.

ESP has no sales team–you’ll work with our producers from the beginning. The event will be easy, because the planning and lead up have been handled—by ESP. You get the credit.
