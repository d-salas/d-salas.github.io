Why Modern Social Secretary?

For over a century, social secretaries have been tending to the correspondence needs of First Ladies, dignitaries and socialites alike. Their simple directives regarding the written word are timeless: be prompt and personal.

Now you can enjoy the same level of service with a 21st century twist. Your Modern Social Secretary will move thank you notes, letters, and invitations off your "to-do" list by helping you put your thoughts into words, onto beautiful stationary, and into the mail.

GET THERE IN 5 EASY STEPS

How it works

While hosting a party, wedding or other event can be exciting, the process of sending the invitations or announcement can be more overwhelming than planning the menu. And then there is the common concern over poor handwriting.

Let Modern Social Secretary take away the stress of thank you notes. We'll provide the text of the correspondence and mail them to your friends and family for you — all you have to do is provide their addresses and let us know who they are and why they deserve your thanks. It almost couldn't be simpler.
