Why MSS?

For over a century, social secretaries have been tending to the correspondence needs of First Ladies, dignitaries and socialites alike. Their simple directives regarding the written word are timeless: be prompt and personal.

Now you can enjoy the same level of service with a 21st century twist. Your Modern Social Secretary will move thank you notes, letters and invitations off your "to-do" list by helping you put your thoughts into words, onto beautiful stationary, and into the mail.
