We make thank you notes so easy, 
they practically write themselves.

For over a century, social secretaries have been tending to the correspondence needs of First Ladies, dignitaries and socialites alike. Their simple directives regarding the written word are timeless: be prompt and personal.

Now you can enjoy the same level of service with a 21st century twist. Your Modern Social Secretary will move thank you notes, letters and invitations off your "to-do" list by helping you put your thoughts into words, and your works onto beautiful stationary and into the mail.

No matter the event, we'll anticipate your every need.

Maybe you want to work around a theme such as an Hogwarts bar mitzvah, or perhaps something as subtle as An Evening in Paris Dinner for friends. Maybe you just want a certain look or a specific sound. Maybe you’d like to have a noisy crowd scene, with ambiance to match, or a perfect setting for intimate conversation? It’s our job to ask questions, to get to know you and to develop a clear vision of what you want your event to be.

We want to develop YOUR concept and then apply our years of event planning and hosting experience so that the proof is in the details. From the music to the visuals to the special enhancements, ESP will bring your concept to life.

ESP’s job is to ensure that your event unfolds just as you envision it and that your guests have a pleasurable and memorable time.

We have no sales team so you’ll work with our producers from the moment you reserve the date until the event is complete. You can rest assured that your function will come off as expected because ESP will handle all the planning down to the smallest details just the way YOU want it. Your guests will leave knowing that it was your party, not ours.
